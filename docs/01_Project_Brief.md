# Vision

To build Africa's most intelligent Founder Operating System that transforms fragmented company knowledge into actionable business intelligence.

JIP enables founders to monitor, understand and improve every aspect of their company—from revenue and compliance to customer feedback, product development, AI insights and strategic decision-making.

Rather than functioning as a dashboard, JIP acts as the institutional memory and intelligence layer of Jiaminie Tech.

---

# Mission

Create a centralized platform where:

• Business knowledge never gets lost.
• Founder decisions are evidence-based.
• Compliance becomes proactive instead of reactive.
• AI assists founders in making better decisions.
• Products improve continuously through customer insights.
• Company operations become measurable and predictable.

# Background

Jiaminie Tech currently operates several software products:

- JChats
- CRM
- POS
- Socials (Pilot)

As the business grows, information becomes scattered across:

- WhatsApp
- Meetings
- GitHub
- Google Docs
- Personal notes
- Emails
- APIs
- Government documentation
- Customer conversations

This fragmentation creates several business risks:

- Forgotten ideas
- Lost customer feedback
- Missed compliance updates
- Duplicate research
- Poor decision traceability
- Reactive instead of proactive management

JIP addresses these challenges by becoming the company's central intelligence platform.

Without structured knowledge management, founders spend increasing amounts of time:

- Searching for information

JIP aims to convert company knowledge into a strategic asset.

# External Data Policy

JIP prioritizes official information sources.

Preferred order:

1. Official APIs
2. RSS Feeds
3. Public documentation
4. Government publications

Web crawling is permitted only when:

- Allowed by the website's Terms of Service.
- Consistent with robots.txt directives where applicable.
- Respectful of rate limits.
- Used for lawful business purposes.

JIP will not:

- Circumvent authentication.
- Bypass paywalls.
- Evade CAPTCHAs.
- Ignore robots exclusions.
- Scrape private content.
- Collect personal data without a lawful basis.
